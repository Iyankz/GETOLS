SNMP Setup Commands

Copy-Paste Helper

Configure SNMP on your ZTE OLT before adding it to GETOLS:

1. SNMP Read-Only Community (Required)
snmp-server community ztero view AllView ro
Replace ztero with your desired RO community string
2. SNMP Read-Write Community (Optional - for ONU Reset)
snmp-server community zterw view AllView rw
Replace zterw with your desired RW community string
Security Notes:
  • SNMP RW is only used for ONU reset operations (Admin only)
  • All SNMP RW usage is logged in Activity Log
  • Use strong, unique community strings
  • Restrict SNMP access via ACL if possible
